Liberwave is a student to student textbook selling app.

The main screen of the app features a location based search using Google Maps and Google Places API.

Students create listings with a beautiful and responsive form. We used Google Books API to autofill the details for known books. Students can also easily take pictures of their book to show the current condition.

We chose a side navigation menu that easily slides in and out of view.

Students are rated after every transaction to ensure accountability.

You can buy and sell multiple books at a time with ease.
